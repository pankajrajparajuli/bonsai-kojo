Golden Cypress Bonsai Tree

$ 18.99
The evergreen golden cypress (Cupressus macrocarpa), which is native to California, is sometimes grown as a bonsai because of its lovely golden-yellow leaf and adaptability. Golden cypress bonsai can be fashioned in a range of bonsai forms, such as formal and casual upright styles, slanting styles, and group plantings. They have a naturally conical habit. They tolerate a broad variety of temperatures and humidity levels but prefer full sun and soil that drains well. If not properly cared for, golden cypress bonsai are susceptible to certain pests and illnesses and require routine trimming and shaping to retain their ideal form.
