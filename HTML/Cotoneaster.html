Cotoneaster Bonsai Tree

$ 98.79
A popular genus of evergreen and deciduous shrubs and tiny trees for bonsai cultivation is Cotoneaster. Its small leaves, lovely foliage, and lovely berries make it a favorite. They may be styled into many different bonsai forms, such as group plantings and cascade designs, and have a spreading, slightly upright habit. Cotoneaster bonsai may grow in a variety of environments, including low light and a range of humidity levels, and are generally simple to maintain. They appreciate soil with good drainage, and to keep their intended shape, they require routine training and trimming. When conditions are ideal, they can develop quickly and in the spring, they can have a large number of small, spectacular white or pink blooms, which are followed by tiny red, orange, or black berries in the fall.
