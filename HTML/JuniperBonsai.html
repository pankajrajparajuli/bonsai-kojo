Juniper Bonsai Tree

$ 190
A specific variety of bonsai tree called a Juniper Bonsai is cultivated from Juniperus coniferous trees and shrubs. It is recognized for its unusual, gnarled stem and its lush, green foliage, which can have a variety of textures from needle-like to scale-like. There are numerous ways to train juniper bonsai, including formal upright, slanting, and cascade. It is a well-liked option among bonsai aficionados because of its hardiness, adaptability, and simplicity of maintenance. A well-draining soil and indirect light are necessary for Juniper Bonsai. To keep it at the proper size and shape, regular training and trimming are required. Chinese Juniper (Juniperus chinensis), Green Mound Juniper (Juniperus procumbens 'Nana'), and Shimpaku Juniper (Juniperus chinensis 'Shimpaku') are a few species that are frequently utilized for Juniper Bonsai.
