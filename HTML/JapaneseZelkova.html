Japanese Zelkova Bonsai Tree

$ 240
A little ornamental tree called a Japanese Zelkova bonsai is cultivated and pruned to keep its diminutive size. It originates from Japan and is a member of the Zelkova genus. A spreading canopy of delicate, serrated leaves and a vase-shaped trunk give the tree its distinctive, attractive shape. Zelkova bonsai from Japan are highly valued for their capacity to create a lovely miniature representation of a full-sized tree in a pot. This makes it a well-liked option for bonsai collectors and aficionados.
